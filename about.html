About

Bongo cat is a cute, white cat that has become a musician for the world to hear. Bongo cat is an animation of a cat playing the bongos

Along with the various songs that Bongo has played, these are some of the instruments other than just the bongos, which bongo cat has played:

  • saxophone
  • rubber chickens
  • tamborins
  • clarinet
  • and the list goes on from there
